# PRD: Goldner & Dias Paes Advocacia - Premium Law Firm Homepage

## Original Problem Statement
Build a premium homepage layout for "Goldner & Dias Paes Advocacia" - a high-end Brazilian law firm with minimal, premium, calm editorial-style design featuring muted gold accents on dark navy background.

## User Personas
- **Primary**: High-net-worth individuals and corporate clients seeking premium legal services
- **Secondary**: Business executives looking for strategic legal counsel

## Core Requirements
- Muted gold accent (#D4AF37) on dark navy (#040914) background
- Full-width hero with user-provided logo
- Single Founder-Partner profile (no team grid)
- 3 office locations (São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, Brasília)
- Social media links in footer
- Portuguese language content
- Mobile responsive design

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React, Tailwind CSS, Lucide React icons
- Styling: Custom CSS with glassmorphism effects
- Fonts: Playfair Display (headings), Outfit (body)

## Design System
- Primary Color: #D4AF37 (Muted Gold)
- Background: #040914 (Dark Navy)
- Surface: #0B1325 (Navy Surface)
- Text: White with varying opacity
- Border: #2A344A with white/5-10% opacity
